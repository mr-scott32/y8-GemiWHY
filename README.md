# y8-GemiWHY
These activities are intended to give you an overview of AI vs Search Engines, and how to perform **critical research** instead of opting for AI use. 

## 1 - Read for Understanding
Read through the following first. To access them, click the files on the left hand side of the screen or click the links below.
* [1- _Artificial Un(Intelligence).md_](https://github.com/mr-scott32/y8-GemiWHY/blob/main/1%20-%20Artificial%20(Un)Intelligence.md "Read this first to understand the difference between AI and a search engine - they are NOT the same thing")
* [2 - _How to Google like a pro.md_](https://github.com/mr-scott32/y8-GemiWHY/blob/main/2%20-%20How%20to%20Google%20like%20a%20pro.md "Read this to understand how you can effectively search for information on the Internet with AI use")

## 2 - Think, Pair, Share
* **Think**: As you finish reading both documents, consider what you think about the information presented. You will have 1-2 minutes to do this. Consider:
  * What is your initial reaction?
  * Have you been sourcing information effectively?
  * Do you regularly consider **where** your information comes from, and **who** is presenting it?
  * Have you tried both AI overview and Google? How do you find they compare?
 
* **Pair**: Discuss the above with someone next to you, or as a small group. You will have 2-3 minutes to do this.

* **Share**: Your teacher will ask for members of each group to share what they have discussed with the rest of the class. This will take 3-5 minutes.
